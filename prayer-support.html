Financial Accountability

We are open and transparent both before God and man in the stewardship of gifts or donations entrusted to us. Each year all our accounts are audited by a government authorized Charted Accountant. We submit our annual financial statement to the Govt. of India and to the donors concerned.
All gifts given are being used appropriately to advance the Gospel of Christ & for the extension of God's Kingdom. Every effort is made to minimize unnecessary administrative costs associate with each gift.
