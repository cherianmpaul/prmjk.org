Our Ministries

A New Day Has Dawned For Those Living In Darkness

"The people walking in darkness have seen a great light, those living in the shadow of death, a light has dawned." (Isaiah9: 2).Literally a new day has dawned in the lives of many that were walking in darkness. Yes, they were once in the clutches of Satan, under the grip of superstitious beliefs. God has outstretched his arms, he has proved himself living and mighty among them with signs and wonders. Bible says, " He raises the poor out of the dust and lifts the needy out of the ash heap that he may seat him with the princes." (Psalm 113:4).Other Sheep, Other Villages & Other Cities (John:10:16, Mark: 1:38, Luke: 4:43.)

But Jesus was always burdened about the untold millions who are still outside, "Other Sheep" I have which are not of this fold, I must bring them also - John: 10:16. He refused to remain in one place. The crowd tried to keep him back because of the many miracles He did. But He left the places saying, "I must preach the kingdom of God to other villages, towns & cities". (Mark: 1:38, Luke: 4:43).

Go... preach ... Teach...Heal... Baptize... And Make Disciples ... of all nations is our Vision and Burden. The aims and objects of our missionary organization are identical with our Vision & Burden. "It has always been my ambition to preach the gospel where Christ was not known, so that I would not be building on someone else foundation .... Those who have not heard will understand". Rom ... 15:-20-21.

Strategy for Finishing the Task

As far as Jammu Kashmir is concerned, there are many people groups which were traditionally closed for centuries for the Gospel are now slowly opening up and "the true light which enlightens every man" starts shining over the prevailing spiritual darkness.

Strategies

1) Prayer mobilization.
2) Net working & partnering.
3) Systematic outreach & Church planting.
4) Systematic Training of Disciples & Leaders.
