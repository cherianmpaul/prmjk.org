Welcome to Penetration & Restoration Ministries, Jammu Kashmir!

PRM stands for penetration of the gospel to the most interior parts, restoring God's people to the apostolic teaching, making disciples and planting house Churches of New Testament pattern.

We are also committed to accomplish the great commission, to reach the unreached, to evangelize the non-evangelized and the holistic development of community.
